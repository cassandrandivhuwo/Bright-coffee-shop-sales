# Bright-coffe-shop-sales
Analysis for Bright coffee shop sales, to understand sales over the last 6 months.

###Problem statement
The sales at the shop have been declining in the past 6 months and the CEO wants to undertand the cause of the decline in sales. I am tasked to do diagnostics analysis to understand the decline in revenue.

### Aim
The aim of this project is to provide detailed insights on what caused the decline in revenue.

### Objectives
In order to achieve the aim, the following objectives must be met:
- Use Miro for brainstorming
- Use Canva for project planning
- Use Databricks for Data Processing

## Summary of results
- The sales dropped by 50% in January as compared to December, this could be due to season changes.
